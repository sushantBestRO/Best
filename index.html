Gap Analysis vs. Peers

Anchor Accounts

What Peers Do:

Grow via repeat orders from large, diverse clients (e.g., BLR).

Our Gap:

Need stronger Key Account Management, quarterly reviews, and expansion plans for top 25 clients.

PTL Network

What Peers Do:

Run dense PTL networks across India (e.g., Inland, SGT).

Our Gap:

Predominantly focused on the Northern Region; need to expand South and East.

Warehousing/3PL Bundling

What Peers Do:

Bundle transport with warehousing/3PL services (e.g., Express, SGT).

Our Gap:

Negligible percentage of clients using more than one service.

Growth at What Cost?

The data presents a clear dilemma. We can chase top-line revenue like our competitors, but the chart shows this often leads to razor-thin—or even negative—profits.

Is aggressive top-line growth worth sacrificing our strong bottom-line?

This is the fundamental question we must answer. Our strategy must define whether we join the "race to the bottom" or forge a path of sustainable, profitable growth, leveraging our high-margin segments.
